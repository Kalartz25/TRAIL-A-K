List your products
Your product listings are the window to your online business. A product listing is equivalent to a salesperson in your store. A good listing can help you build customer trust, enable them to make an informed purchase decision and even help in reducing customer returns or escalations. It is natural to be a little overwhelmed when you are listing your products online for the first time. However, with our easy and intuitive listing template, you can list unlimited products in a beat. All you have to do is submit a few simple details like product name, images, category, price, size/ colour attributes for variations (if any) and you are done!
Know how to list your products on SmartBiz or download our e-book if you fancy a little takeaway reading.
Bonus: Here is a blog with tips on great product photography on a budget for small businesses. You can also head over to our YouTube channel to watch this video on how to write the perfect product description.
Design your website the way you want
At SmartBiz, we recognize the individuality of each brand. That's why we provide you with various options to tailor your website's appearance, enabling you to create your website to truly reflect your brand. In order to do this, you don't need design skills. Play around with various theme colours, background themes, headers, banners etc. You can either choose from our predefined templates for these elements or even upload your own images. Sounds fun? We assure you it is! Learn how to customize your website appearance.
Setup payments
Let’s now talk about the most exciting part of your business: getting paid! Tracking and managing payments from your customers without a website can be extremely cumbersome and tedious. With SmartBiz, you can simplify your payments process, ensuring that you can run your business smoothly and without any worries. You need to complete your KYC with the payment gateway and once done, you can start offering multiple online payment options to your customers. With timely payments and 2-day settlements for your online sales through your website, you can focus on what matters most - growing your business hassle-free.
Setup delivery and shipping
Securing that first purchase from your customers on your all-new website can be challenging. In today's world of online shopping, offering free delivery has become an increasingly popular and coveted choice among shoppers. According to a survey conducted by us, as many as 66% of consumers say free shipping makes them more likely to shop. Shipping costs are perceived as an additional barrier to online shopping convenience, and customers are increasingly expecting free shipping on their orders. Therefore, as a new seller who is starting their online business, you may want to consider offering free deliveries for your online orders. However, if it is unavailable for your business, you can set minimum delivery fees for your orders. SmartBiz enables you to set up shipping fees or offer free delivery for all your orders. You can also set up free delivery on a minimum cart value.

Processing your orders
You received your first order. What’s next? Processing and fulfilling these orders. With SmartBiz by Amazon, you now no longer need to keep track of multiple DMs and conversation threads and can process and manage all your orders in one place. You can find all orders that you receive at one place, accept or decline these orders and then proceed to ship these out by yourself or by using our shipping services powered by Shiprocket. Ensure that you promptly and swiftly process all your orders so as to delight your customers. Here are some tips to help you do just that.
Shipping your orders
One of the most important factors in running an e-commerce business is your logistics strategy. On your website built using SmartBiz, customers can choose to receive their orders in 2 ways- i) in-store pick-ups where they order online but pick up their order in person from your physical location such as a store, and ii) home delivery - where they expect their order to be delivered to their doorstep. You can choose to enable or disable either of these options as per your business strategy. For home delivery orders, you can choose to ship it directly with your own preferred logistics partner. Alternatively, you can use our integration with Shiprocket to find the right logistics partner for that delivery. Within the app, you will be able to compare between all the available logistics partners on Shiprocket and deliver your orders with directly. Click here to know how you can set up your Shiprocket account and avail integrated shipping services.
