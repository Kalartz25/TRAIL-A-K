Frequently Asked Questions

What is SmartBiz by Amazon?
SmartBiz by Amazon is your one-stop shop for all your online selling needs. Whether you are a small business, an entrepreneur, or a neighborhood store, you can now build website for ecommerce within minutes and start showcasing and selling your products online. What's more is that you do not need any technical knowledge to get started online. Just download the SmartBiz by Amazon app and try it completely for free until March 2025.
Try for free
I am interested. Now can I start using SmartBiz by Amazon?
You can build your own e-commerce store in minutes on SmartBiz by Amazon. All you need to do is follow these simple steps:
  • Download the SmartBiz by Amazon app and set up your seller account.
  • List all your products online and create an online catalog of your products
  • Set up your payment gateway and shipping services partners as required
  • Share your website link with your customers and start receiving online orders
  • Receive payments for your sales within 2 days
  • Fulfill your orders using self-ship, home delivery, or in-store pick ups
Know more
Do I need GST to start selling using SmartBiz by Amazon?
You can start selling your products online on your own online store created using SmartBiz without any GST. However, in order to enable inter-state shipping, you will need GST. Therefore, although optional for selling within the state, it is recommended to get a GST number in order to grow your business online and reach customers across India. Click here to know everything about GST.
What are the charges or fees for using SmartBiz by Amazon?
There are no charges, fees, or commission currently for using SmartBiz. As an introductory offer until March 2025, you can create and sell using your online store at zero cost. Just download the SmartBiz app and start your free trial.
Try for free
How can I create a catalog and list my products online?
Listing your product online has never been this easy. Simply click pictures of your product (you can do this even on your phone) and add details about your product category and price. You are done! Click here to know more.
How can I promote my online store to customers?
You can promote your online store to your prospective and existing customers by
  • Sharing your store URL on social media, Whatsapp, and other online platforms
  • Integrating with a chat commerce solution to send promotional messages
  • Sharing individual product links from your catalog
  • Sharing the QR code online as well as on offline platforms (such as in your physical store, via pamphlets, brochures, and posters)
Click here to know more.
How can I ship my orders?
You can enable in-store pick up options as well as home delivery options for your customers, and let them choose as per their convenience. For home deliveries, you can choose any carrier offered by Shiprocket for doorstep pick up and delivery. You can also opt for self-shipping and choose your own courier partner or deliver it yourself. Click here to know more.
How will I get paid?
SmartBiz ensures that all your financial transactions are smooth and efficient. You can set up your online payment gateway using Razorpay/ Pine Labs and offer multiple online payment options for customers. Thereafter, you can start accepting prepaid digital payments for your sales. Click here to know more.
How do I get help?
At any time, if you need help in setting up or operating your online store, please refer to the help center on our website to access FAQs and other useful resources such as blogs, tutorials, and step-by-step explainers. In case you need more assistance, you can reach out to us at smartbiz-support@amazon.com. You can also connect with us on social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube.
